Social Media Optimization (SMO)

SMO is a valuable tool in marketing a business and creating more visibility for a website. When thinking about web design, it’s important to consider ways to make a website optimized for good SMO. One method of creating a social media optimized website, is adding links for sharing content for social bookmarking. Another thing to keep in mind when designing a website is installing site plug-ins.

According to Lifewire, “Social bookmarking is an online service which allows you to create and publicly share website bookmarks with other members of a community by simply tagging a web page with a web-based tool so you can easily access it later” (Nations). Big social bookmarking sites include Twitter, Reddit and Pinterest, and to optimize a website for social bookmarking would allow for large social media marketing opportunities. “Increase traffic by adding social bookmarking buttons to your website. Adding social bookmark links to your blog or website makes it easy for readers to save and share your content, and therefore for others to find it” (Network Solutions). With this being said, it is important to design a website that makes it easy for users to share website content to social media to increase social media marketing.

When designing a website, a useful way to build SMO is using plug-ins. A plug-in is a piece of software that is downloaded to add more functionality to a website. For example, a plug-in like the Facebook Like Button for the Web, can be installed to a website and allow users to easily ‘like’ content on your website and have it automatically show on their profile. “A single click on the Like button will 'like' pieces of content on the web and share them on Facebook. You can also display a Share button next to the Like button to let people add a personal message and customize who they share with” (Facebook). A plug-in like the Facebook Like button would keep users engaged with your content, and provide free marketing for your site.
