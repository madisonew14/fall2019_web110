Accessibility

As a web designer, it’s important to know how to create an accessible website for all. Not only is it simply the right thing to do, but making websites accessible to those with disabilities come with many great business benefits. Creating a website that has good accessibility comes hand-in-hand with good SEO within search engines. Lastly, when you create a website for a business that is accessible, it opens up a wider range of consumers.

Creating a website that is accessible to those with disabilities comes with great SEO benefits. For example, when a site has a descriptive title of the web content, it’s the first thing read to a user using a screen reader. It gives them a good idea of what’s on the website and can help them quickly identify if the content is what they’re looking for. But including a descriptive title has SEO benefits, because it will determine where the site will appear in a search engine. This can greatly affect the amount of clicks the website will get.

“According to Moz, “Your title tag determines (with a few exceptions) your display title in SERPs [search engine results pages], and is a search visitor’s first experience of your site. Even if your site ranks well, a good title can be the make-or-break factor in determining whether or not someone clicks on your link.” This means that in almost every scenario, writing a title that accurately reflects page content is a win-win.” (2019 DBS Interactive) Creating a descriptive site title is an example of how good user accessibility creates better business opportunities through SEO.

Lastly, there is more business opportunity for companies with accessible websites than those who don’t. According to W3, “In the US, the annual discretionary spending of people with disabilities is over $200 billion. The global estimate of the disability market is nearly $7 trillion.” (2018 W3C) Potential consumers could have disabilities, and having a website that isn’t accessible to them is business that can be lost. Having an accessible consumer website, allows a company to reach a larger consumer demographics.
