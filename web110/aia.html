AIA (Audience, Issues and Approach)

Client Information

Client's Name Thomas Pantig (friend)
Business Name Thomas Pantig Photography
Redesign or Brand New? Redesign of current website
Type of Website Photography business
Client Goals To create a website that is easy to navigate and can attract more and a broader range of clientele

Audience Issues, Concerns Approach
Event Planners/Managers:
The age of this type of client could range anywhere between early twenties to 65. However, no matter the age, it can be assumed that advanced computer and social media skills are needed for someone in this position. Event managers will be looking at this website to see examples of event photography, and potentially a way to contact Thomas. Since many event managers work in an office, they’re most likely viewing this website on a large screen, but they’re often on the go, so it's possibly the website is being viewed on a mobile phone.
  • Will need a way to contact Thomas such as a contact page
  • Needs to see portfolio- particularily photos from events, such as action shots for social media posts and other marketing purposes
  • Would need to ensure that Thomas is the right photographer for their event and provides good customer service and quality work
  • Might want to know if he is able to travel
  • Would find social media pages useful to see more examples of work
  • Contact page
  • Would be beneficial to have page that outlines all rates, possible packages and the rates for editing time
  • Needs to have external links to social media pages like Instagram (to see possible examples of event photography
  • Show past clients/testimonials
  • Possible FAQ page
Parents:
Parents are a potential client looking at this website for senior photos for their high school children. They’re of the older demographic so this group could be the least experience with using computers, and most likely to be viewing the website on a desktop.
  • Will need a way to contact Thomas such as a contact page
  • Hourly rates for onsite shooting and editing time and/or packages
  • Needs to see examples of portrait work
  • Needs to know if Thomas can do studio photos or outdoor only
  • Since they are the older generation, may not know how to navigate a website as well as the other audiences- website needs to be easy to navigate
  • Easy and clear contact page
  • Easy and visible navigation bar
  • Clear laid out pricing rates in a easy to read table
  • Include an FAQ page
  • Include embedded Google Map and working hours
  • Contact page rather than mailto link
  • Include testimonial page of past clients
Young adults:
This group is most likely looking for a photographer to do professional headshots, engagement, and maternity photos. It’s equally possible they could be viewing the website on a desktop or smaller screen like a smartphone or tablet. They’re the most skilled at navigating websites and the internet. Young adults are frequent users of social media and will most likely have social media apps on their phones.
  • Contact information for Thomas
  • Hourly rate for onsite shooting and editing time
  • Needs examples of past portraits, maternity and/or engagement photos
  • Will need to know if studio shoots are doable
  • Highly likely they're viewing the website on a mobile device, so site needs to be mobile friendly
  • Add easily accessible links to social media pages to see photography portfolio (this audience will most likely have apps already installed on their phone, so this external link would bring them to the app)
  • About me page to get a better sense of who Thomas is.
  • Add a page dedicated for couples pictures
  • Testimonials to see what other clients thought of Thomas
